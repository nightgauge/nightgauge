# Product Context

Use this page to record the public purpose and user-visible boundaries of the
product being developed.

## Users

- Who uses the product?
- What problem are they trying to solve?
- What constraints or accessibility needs matter?

## Value

- What outcome does the product enable?
- Which capabilities are intentionally in scope?
- Which capabilities are explicitly out of scope?

Keep private commercial strategy, customer identities, credentials, and
confidential roadmap information out of repository knowledge bases.
